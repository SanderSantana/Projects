Thoughts of a Carnal man

by Sander Santana

I feel at ease when I'm with you. When in your presence, my problems evaporate and your thoughts condense like little water droplets in my mind, I feel me feel whole.

Why do you keep blessing the wicked man? Why do you love the crafty man? Why do you protect the unrighteous man? Whose heart isn't clean and whose tongue has not control off.

Please don't throw me out Please don't get rid of me If you do I'll be like those whose heart feeds on wickedness and whose mind is consumed with evil.

Yes: my heart leans on wickedness too, but don't let be like them. Let me look more like those who walk on the narrow path. Those who delight in hardships and Adversities. Those who pay bad with good and wrap their actions with courtesy. Those who proclaim your name without fear and trust. Let me be your child. Let me be your servant. Let me be your worshiper. I want to be a Christian.
